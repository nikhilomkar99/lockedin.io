COVID-19

COVID-19 or Coronavirus is caused SARS-CoV-2.It targets the respiratory system of the patient ,reducing the exchange of oxygen in lungs. A person can be infected by the virus if he/she comes in close contact with an infected person when he coughs or sneezes spreading droplets in the air.It can also be transmitted indirectly when the person touches the surfaces where droplets might have fallen.

Origins

Virus originated from Wuhan,China.It is said that it spread from the wet market(market where liquid is used to freshen the perishable items) called Huanan Seafood Wholesale Market where daily goods like fruit,vegitables and meat.

BASIC RULES

Follow social distancing.

Wash your hands at regular interval.

Work from home.

Cover your cough.

Wear mask when going out.

Contact authorities if signs of symptoms occur.
